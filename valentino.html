PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

Valentino Uomo Born In Roma Intense by Valentino is a Amber Vanilla fragrance for men. This is a new fragrance. Valentino Uomo Born In Roma Intense was launched in
2023. Valentino Uomo Born In Roma Intense was created by Antoine Maisondieu and Guillaume Flavigny.

Born In Roma Uomo Intense is a seductive, ambery fougère fragrance, combining lavandin and vanilla infusion into a hypnotic, sensual aura.

The Valentino Born In Roma fragrance collection is an outstanding tribute to Rome, intertwining Valentino’s couture elegance with edgy modernity, reminiscent of Rome’s
street culture. The Born In Roma story continues with Born In Roma Intense. This fragrance is created to celebrate a night in the eternal city, amplifying the Born In
Roma addiction with a powerful vanilla infusion. This unsweetened and leathery note is intensified by the Heart of Vetiver and lifted with the floral and aromatic
freshness of lavandin. This creates a playful duel between an explosion of freshness, and deep sensuality.

Born In Roma Intense allows vibrant individuals to express themselves with intensity, abundance and duality. This Valentino fragrance for men is presented in an
iconic studded bottle, a fashion signature of Valentino Couture, inspired by Roman architecture, and representing a rebellious new era for high fashion - led by
the fashion house itself.

The Born In Roma collection is created as a duo, to celebrate all magnificent personalities and pairings, with Uomo (for him) and Donna (for her). This iconic duo
playfully challenges the rivalry between day and night, classic and intensity, and you and me.
